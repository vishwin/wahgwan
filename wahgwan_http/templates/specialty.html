Specialty work

Great ideas and projects need great implementation. Sometimes, even breaking down requirements to fundamentals, you may end up in a bind with regards to expertise for a specific feature. Or, you may not have even begun to (re-)evaluate your user experience cycle. We can help!

We are backed by experience hacking in over twenty hackathons since 2013 on a variety of projects for different subjects and industries, pushing the limits of what can come to life over the course of a weekend. Both software and hardware projects are well represented. Some specific projects:

  • a mobile-first web application enabling pre-orders of food and drink whilst planning a long training cycle ride
  • menstrual cramp-alleviating undergarments
  • extensible internet-connected hot tub framework
  • for use during childbirth, perineal massaging device prototype

As such, our capabilities include but are not limited to:

  • Bluetooth, classic and Low Energy
  • software development on embedded Unix-like platforms
  • countless Raspberry Pis, BeagleBoards/BeagleBones, Arduinos and other single-board computers and microcontrollers
  • mobile soldering and desoldering kit
  • assorted electronic parts in stock and supply chain

Contact us today to find out how we can help out with your project.
